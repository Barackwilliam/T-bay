T-bay Tanzania

Welcome to T-bay – Your Premium Marketplace from Regions to Cities

T-bay is an online commercial platform designed for Tanzanians, enabling easy, secure, and affordable buying, selling, and distribution of various products. We combine the power of technology with an understanding of the Tanzanian market to create opportunities for customers and sellers.

We help small, medium, and large businesses reach more customers and provide citizens with the products they need without the barrier of distance. T-bay is more than e-commerce — it is a digital economic revolution putting Tanzania ahead.

Vision

To be the most trusted online marketplace in Tanzania, connecting sellers and buyers digitally and strengthening the local economy through technology.

Mission

To provide easy, secure, and patriotic online buying and selling services for Tanzanians, empowering local businesses and reducing the gap in product availability for urban and rural residents.

How T-bay Works

T-bay is designed to be simple for everyone, whether a customer or a seller.

1. Register

Customers or sellers register accounts using phone or email. We keep registration simple and verify via SMS or email.

2. Upload Products

Sellers can add their products with images, descriptions, prices, and shipping details. The system supports a variety of products, from clothing, food, home goods to electronics.

3. Buy / Order

Buyers can search, compare, and place orders for products. Each order is tracked in real-time, and customers receive updates on shipping progress.

4. Delivery

T-bay partners with delivery service providers within Tanzania to ensure products reach buyers quickly and safely.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Can I buy as a customer without an account?

Yes, you can browse products, but to place an order, you need to register so we can track your order and send shipping updates.

Can local sellers sell without high fees?

T-bay has a fee structure designed to support small businesses; there are different plans depending on seller size and sales volume.

How can I track my order?

After placing an order, you will receive a tracking number; visit the "My Orders" section on your dashboard to see shipping status.

Can I return products?

Yes—there is a 7-day return policy if the product does not match the description, following the return procedures in the system.
